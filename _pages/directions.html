DIRECTIONS TO CASEY INDUSTRIAL PARK
(STRUTHERS, OH)

***CARS ONLY***

(FROM PITTSBURGH)
Distance: 63.4 miles (about 1 hour 17 mins)

1. I-76 (PA TURNPIKE) to Ohio Turnpike to Exit 234 (680)
2. Head north on 680 to Exit 8 (Shirley Road)
3. Bear right onto Shirley Road to the bottom of the hill
4. Bear right onto Poland Avenue - go 1.5 mi
5. Turn left at Walton Street and cross bridge
6. Bear right at stop sign
7. Continue to Casey Industrial Park.


(FROM PITTSBURGH INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT)

1. Start out going NORTHWEST 0.8 miles
2. Merge onto PA-60 N toward MOON / BEAVER / CARGO / PENNSYLVANIA TURNPIKE WEST 21.6 miles
3. Merge onto BEAVER VALLEY EXPY / PA-60 TOLL N (Portions toll) 5.6 miles
4. Merge onto I-76 W via EXIT 33 toward OHIO (Portions toll) 17.2 miles
5. Follow to Ohio turnpike to first exit, Exit 234 (680)
6. Head north on 680 to Exit 8 (Shirley Road)
7. Bear right onto Shirley Road to the bottom of the hill
8. Bear right onto Poland Avenue - go 1.5 mi
9. Turn left at Walton Street and cross bridge
10. Bear right at end of bridge
11. Continue to follow the road into Casey Industrial Park
12. Park and enter first building, past entrance, on the right
